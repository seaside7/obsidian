# The Correct Logic

Prime slots should be determined by:

**Total demand ÷ expected leads per Prime listing** (berapa expectednya)

Because each Prime owner must still get **strong ROI** at least roi perception

# Example

City traffic generates:

5,000 leads/month.

If a Prime owner needs about:

50 leads/month to justify subscription,

Then:

5,000 ÷ 50 = **100 Prime slots**

So the city should have about **100 Prime listings**.
